Tykhe Profile Designs

Tykhe is a game project that me and my friends have been working on for the past year or so, and is a digital card-collecting game on the platform Discord, and it currently has over 4.5 thousand players. The cards contain characters from many different entertainment franchises and media, from games, movies, and TV shows.

My role in this project is visual design, with one of my biggest contributions being profile templates. These profiles are where players can customize and set their personal favorite cards on to display.

Profiles come in various colors and themes, and these themes draw visual reference from a few franchises.
