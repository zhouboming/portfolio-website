Introduction

My name is Boming Zhou and I am a junior UX designer. I’ve been invested in many artistic mediums throughout my whole life and I’ve always enjoyed all of them, such as drawing, graphic design, and video editing, so artistic expression and education are things that have driven my creative side for years, and I wanted to employ these skills into UX design. I also have a bachelor’s in Psychology from UMass Amherst, so I value the importance of sympathy and understanding perspectives of others. So with all this in mind, I seek to combine all these skills and utilize them perfectly for UX design, where products are created from the research and understandings of the users, and designed with utmost aesthetics and functionality.
