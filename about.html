Introduction

My name is Boming Zhou and I am a junior UX designer. I’ve been invested in many artistic mediums throughout my whole life, from drawing, graphic design, to video editing, so artistic expression and education are things that have driven my creative side for years, and I seek to employ these skills into UX design.

With a bachelor’s in Psychology, I value the importance of sympathy and understanding perspectives of others. So with all this in mind, I seek to combine all these skills and utilize them perfectly for UX design, where products are created from the research and understandings of the users, and designed with utmost aesthetics and functionality.
