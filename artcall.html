ArtCall

ArtCall is an app I designed for my main CareerFoundry UX Immersion course from January 2021 to July 2021, where I continuously learned more about the UX design process and applied my learnings onto the app.

Its goal is to connect users with proffesional artists and designers through scheduled video calls in order to improve their own skills, gain more artistic knowledge, and grow their own art community.
